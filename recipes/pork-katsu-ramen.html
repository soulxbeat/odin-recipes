Pork Katsu Ramen

Description:

This is one of my favorite dishes! The broth is simmered with green onions, garlic, ginger, soy sauce and mirin. Just add your favorite noodles and toppings. I like to use mushrooms, bok choy, daikon radish and red peppers. If you're feeling adventurous, add some chili paste or fresh chopped chili peppers or jalapenoes!

Ingredients:

  • Thin sliced pork loin chops (for as many bowls as you want to make)
  • Veggies (daikon, red pepper, shiitake mushrooms, bok choy)
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon mirin
  • soba or ramen style noodles
  • salt
  • pepper
